Canal Maintenance Timeline

This timeline contains information collected by the Venice Project Center on canals maintenance.
The canal maintenance information was provided by INSULA
To use drag the slider on the bottom right to change the displayed time. The map will update with segments being dredged at that time in yellow and canals that have had past maintenance in green. Segments in red have not had recorded maintenance before the selected time
The timeline was developed by the Venice Project Center 2013 Canals Team
